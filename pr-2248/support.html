Support

Get help with Qiskit and IBM Quantum Platform

IBM Quantum™ Network membersReach out to IBM Quantum Support if you have technical difficulties, questions, or concerns. Ask your administrator or IBM® representative for IBM Quantum Support contact information.
Pay-As-You-Go usersOpen a case in the IBM Cloud® Support Center by filling out this form, and choosing 'Qiskit Runtime' from the Topic field.
Open Plan usersAccess the Qiskit Slack community, where you will find topical channels such as #qiskit-runtime, #ibm-quantum-platform, #qiskit-transpiler, #qiskit-ecosystem, and more.

Discuss and ask questions

Discussions on quantum information science and development with the larger quantum computing fieldVisit Quantum Computing Stack Exchange site. Be sure to read the "How do I ask a good question?" guide, and make use of tags such as qiskit, ibm-runtime, and ibm-quantum-services for best results.
Questions specific to programmingVisit Stack Overflow and use the tag qiskit.
